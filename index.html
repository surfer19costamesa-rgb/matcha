The Philosophy of MORITA SABO

During the Azuchi-Momoyama period, tea culture reached its peak as a symbol of prestige and spiritual discipline for the Samurai. Sen no Rikyu perfected the "Sabo" (Tea House) as a sacred space where one could find their true self. We carry this pride in our name. In today's restless world, we offer you a moment of Zen—a place to rest your soul and find tranquility through every bowl of tea.
